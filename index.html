Nediljko Vojković

More than thirty years numerous decorations, reliefs or pictures decorate many homes and offices in Croatia and through the world.

Art studio's leader Nediljko Vojkovic was born 1952. in Dicmo, twenty kilometers from Split.He is very expirienced creator originate from his profession of designer of industrial design and also the interior design.His work was recognized long time ago as uneffected, recognized style and by clean form of work.His art activity originate from his own acting, materialized by mind visualisation with no outer force, therefore created in the intimacy of his own art emotion for creating beauty.

Recognized originality of the Mediterian mood and also very resonable prices make this art studio very creative and very well visited.

We thank you for your visit and have nice moments while you are watching our pictures.
